About the Studio

Two crafts.
One vision.

Studio|Diah is a creative space rooted in presence, precision, and the art of transformation. Every experience begins with a vision and ends with something that stays with you.

Based between Seattle and Los Angeles, the studio operates across disciplines — each one its own world, each one unmistakably Diah.
